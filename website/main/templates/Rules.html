FarmSim Society Rules

Click on each section to expand and read the rules:

  • Punishments may escalate based on the severity of the violation.
  • Staff can apply punishments not explicitly listed (e.g., muting, fines, or command restrictions).

  • Do not DM or tag staff.
  • No NSFW, ads, spam, bullying, or hate. Respect everyone—follow the Golden Rule!
  • No griefing or trolling.
  • No multiple Discord accounts or ban evasion. Use a ticket to swap accounts.
  • English only in Discord and in-game chats.

  • Stay active: 30+ days of inactivity = farm slot forfeited. Engage in gameplay to remain active.
  • No lending money: Includes unpaid loans, trades, or payment plans with <15% down.
  • No gifting assets: Examples: free leases, underpriced sales, or trading for labor.
  • Contracts: One at a time, complete ASAP—no delays or breaks.
  • No exploiting bugs, cheats, or glitches. Report issues via ticket.
  • No transfer abuse: No crop hopping or duplicating vehicles across servers.

  • Use formal agreements: Protect both parties—be clear about terms.
  • No unauthorized changes to leased land: Examples: landscaping or field expansion.
  • No sub-leasing or sharecropping land you’re leasing.
  • Land limits: Max 200 acres across leased or owned land.

  • Terraform responsibly: Admin fixes = $50k/15 min.
  • No unauthorized building placements on land you don’t own.
  • Field expansion: No combining fields across parcels. Leave 5m from boundaries.
  • Building placement: Leave 5m between fields, parcels, and roads.
  • Farm Slot limits:
    • 3 greenhouses, 5 bee houses, 1 factory per type (exceptions: TMR, Pig Food, Fermenting).
    • Max 6 animal pen points (3 pens max per animal type).
